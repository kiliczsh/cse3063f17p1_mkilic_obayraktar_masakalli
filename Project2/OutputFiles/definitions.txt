1- tf_list.csv: Most frequent 50 words in the input set of documents, sorted descending
by their term frequency (tf) coupled with their tf values (comma seperated file,
example: document;7)
2- tf_wordCloud.pdf: Word cloud of the these words
3- tfidf_list.csv: Most frequent 50 words in the input set of documents, sorted
descending by their term frequency*inverse document frequency (tf-idf) coupled
with their tf-idf values (comma seperated file, example: document;2.8)
4- tfidf_wordCloud.pdf: Word cloud of the these words
